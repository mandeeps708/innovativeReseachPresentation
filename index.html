Introduction

This is something where two big things collaborate for mega invention. A person with innovative idea research on the resources and technologies to convert the idea into live project.

What is Innovation?

The process of translating an idea or invention into a good or services that creates value or for which customers will pay.

What is Research?

Research is a careful and detailed study into a specific problem, concern, or issue using the scientific method.

Why to Research?

  • To solve an existing problem.
  • To improve the performance of a product or process.
  • To benefit the society in the long run. Research is often about finding questions than answers.

Prerequisites for Research

  • Very good understanding of the area
  • Familiarity with tools and methods
  • Imagination and visualization
  • Balanced curiosity
  • Lateral thinking
  • Intuition
